basil pesto

Description

This basil pesto is a new addition to our kitchen this year.

Until two months ago, I had never had pesto of any kind, though I had been wanting to try it for years. I finally got around to make it and I am now obsessed.

Ingredients

  • one container of fresh basil leaves
  • one bag of pine nuts
  • 3/4 cup of olive oil (the better the quality the less you'll need)
  • 5 cloves of garlic, or to taste (this will add heat)
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. To a food processor, add all of the ingredients with half of the oil
  2. Blend the pesto and add oil accordingly, until desired consistency is reached, thick like a paste or runny like a sauce
  3. Serve fresh and refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to three days
