Quick and Easy Homemade Whipped Cream

Description

This is one of the simplest ways to make homemade whipped cream, without the hassle of bringing out the mixer! All it takes is a few minutes and a willing arm.

Ingredients

  • Mason jar with lid (jar big enough to leave half empty)
  • 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • Powdered sugar to taste (normal sugar works fine too!)
  • 1tsp vanilla paste or vanilla extract (optional)

Directions

  1. Pour the ingredients into the mason jar and cover tight with lid
  2. Shake until you can't feel the liquid moving inside (about 3 minutes of constant motion)
  3. Open jar and check the texture. If the cream is too runny, keep shaking!*
  4. Once the desired texture is reached, serve and eat within the next 20 minutes.

You can leave the whipped cream in the jar and shake periodically to keep the texture from softening.

*Make sure you don't over-shake the cream! Doing so will turn the whipped cream into butter.
